Theatre Ops

I was a member of the Board of Directors of a live theatre.  Everything was on paper when it comes to memberships - and that paper would disappear quite often.  Being a programmer, I decided to start writing software that can be used for theatre operations.   Yes, inventory too!
Yes, I know it is ugly, but focus was on having run-able software.  I plan on making it a little more easier on the eyes. 

But inventory is worth money and stuff disappears as shows start because they all grab what they think they need.  So we'll have a check out of inventory to show.  Plus, it'll help ID what is the theatre's and what is theirs. :)

I know of other theaters that are missing this kind of software - basically a mix of CRM and Inventory/Show allocation.  If you want to help, contact scott _ auge @ yahoo . com  -- Scott Auge
